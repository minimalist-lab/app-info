2. Information We Collect

Boki Quest does not require its own account registration and does not transmit any personal information to the operator's servers.

Your learning progress, journal entries, and drill history are stored only in the following locations:

  • On your device (the app's sandbox container)
  • iCloud Drive linked to your Apple ID, if iCloud is enabled

3. iCloud Sync

When iCloud is enabled, your learning data is automatically stored in iCloud Drive to sync across your devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Data is encrypted and managed by Apple's infrastructure; the operator cannot access it.

If you prefer not to use iCloud, turn off Boki Quest under iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > iCloud Drive. Data will then be stored only on your device.

4. In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Boki Quest does not collect or store your payment information.

5. Analytics and Advertising

Boki Quest does not use any third-party analytics tools or advertising SDKs.

6. Deleting Your Data

  • Deleting the app from your device removes locally stored data.
  • Data in iCloud Drive remains. To delete it completely, go to iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage > Boki Quest and choose "Delete Data."

7. Children

Boki Quest does not knowingly collect personal data from children. The app does not require any registration and operates entirely on-device or in the user's own iCloud.

We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
